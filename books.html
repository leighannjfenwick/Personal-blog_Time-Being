A Tale for the Time Being

by Ruth Ozeki

I savoured this book; it took me to different planes of existence.

“A time being is someone who lives in time, and that means you, and me, and every one of us who is, or was, or ever will be.”

Onyx Storm

by Rebecca Yarros

The third book in the trilogy. After reading Fourth Wing and Iron Flame back-to-back, had to wait over a year for this trilogy. Iron Flame ended in such suspense, I almost couldn't stand the wait! I would recommend setting aside time to binge-read this trilogy, and be prepared for some late nights reading.
