A Tale for the Time Being

by Ruth Ozeki

I savoured this book; it took me to different planes of existence.

“A time being is someone who lives in time, and that means you, and me, and every one of us who is, or was, or ever will be.”

Onyx Storm

by Rebecca Yarros

The third book in the trilogy. After reading Fourth Wing and Iron Flame back-to-back, had to wait over a year for this trilogy. Iron Flame ended in such suspense, I almost couldn't stand the wait!

“What the actual fuck?”

Sunrise on the Reaping

by Suzanne Collins

The fifth book in the series. Lives up to the series--describes the second quarter quell and how Haymitch came to be a mentor for Katniss Everdeen in The Hunger Games.

“I'm nobody's idea of a hero, Haymitch. But at least I'm still in the game?” ~Plutarch
