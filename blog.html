January 2026

Smithers, British Columbia

Daylight check-in

The days are getting infinitesimally longer. Here in Smithers today, sunrise is at 8:51 a.m. and sunset at 4:17 p.m. We still have a ways to go before the light really returns, but the daylight minutes are ticking up — slowly and steadily.

A thought for the moment

As we move into a new year (and 2026 has a nice, even ring to it), instead of making resolutions, I’ve been thinking about what I can let go of to feel a little lighter.

I recently came across a line from Mel Robbins about peace coming from releasing the need to control what was never ours to carry in the first place. That idea has been staying with me. I’m trying to loosen my grip on the belief that everything needs to be earned the hard way, and to make a bit more room for ease.
