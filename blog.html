Daylight Minutes

January 2026

Smithers, British Columbia

Daylight check-in

The days are getting slightly longer. Here in Smithers today, sunrise is at 8:51 a.m. and sunset at 4:17 p.m. Light is slowly returning, minute by minute.

A thought for the moment

As we move into 2026, instead of resolutions, I’m considering what I can let go of to feel lighter. A line from Mel Robbins has stayed with me: peace comes from releasing the need to control what was never ours to carry. I’m trying to make room for more ease and lightness.
